How to Get Started

Curious makes exploring exhibits fun and interactive! Follow these simple steps to begin your journey:

  1. Locate a QR code or NFC tag at an exhibit.
  2. Scan the QR code with your phone's camera or tap the NFC tag.
  3. Explore detailed information about the exhibit and the animals within!

Curious works on any modern smartphone with no need to download additional apps.
