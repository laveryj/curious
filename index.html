What is Curious?

Curious is a modern, interactive digital interpretation system designed specifically for zoos and aquariums. It allows visitors to explore detailed information about animals and exhibits by scanning QR codes or tapping NFC tags with their phones.

How Does It Work?

Visitors simply scan a QR code or tap an NFC tag at an exhibit. They are instantly directed to a dedicated page with information about the exhibit, including detailed species profiles, photos, and fun facts.

Why Choose Curious?

  • Accessible: Works on any smartphone without requiring additional apps.
  • Customizable: Fully customizable for each zoo or aquarium, with your branding.
  • Sustainability: Reduces the need for printed materials, promoting eco-friendly practices.
  • Interactive: Provides engaging content such as images, videos, and conservation status.
