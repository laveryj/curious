Welcome to Curious

Curious is a digital interpretation system specifically designed for zoos and aquariums. Our innovative platform empowers your visitors to discover detailed information about animals in your exhibits using their own smartphones by scanning QR codes or tapping NFC tags. It's simple to set up, easy to use, and low-cost.


How Does It Work?

Curious bridges the gap between curiosity and knowledge. Visitors scan a QR code or tap an NFC tag at an exhibit to unlock a treasure trove of information. Each page provides:

  • Detailed species profiles that include images and scientific data.
  • Fascinating fun facts, up-to-date conservation status, and rich images.
  • Optional audio-only mode to create a quick and easy audio guide for your site.

There's no need for app downloads – it works seamlessly on any modern smartphone using the built-in web browser.

Why Choose Curious?

  • Seamless Integration: Works on any smartphone with no app downloads required.
  • Brand Customisation: Tailor the experience to your zoo or aquarium with your own branding.
  • Stay on the pulse: Moved an animal between exhibits? Curious updates in realtime, no more printing new signage!
  • Eco-Friendly: Reduce reliance on printed materials, promoting sustainability.
  • Engaging Content: Offer multimedia-rich profiles with images, videos, audio, and conservation data.
  • Data-Driven Insights: Get analytics on visitor engagement and page views to refine your exhibits.
